Electron capture is similar to Beta decay in that there are allowed (Singlet-like) and disallowed (Triplet-like) transitions, as well as some very low order disallowed contributions. These are, of course, different from Beta decay since we need to consider the total angular momentum of the initial (#tex2html_wrap_inline1157# and final #math106##tex2html_wrap_inline1159# states.
