Klein Paradox

Klein noted that a relativistic (Dirac) particle-in-a-box will leak out at box sizes near the Compton wavelength; this called the Klein paradox [#klein1##1###,#klein2##1###]. And while this is usually taught as being simply particle-antiparticle creation, it has been suggested that the Klein paradox it can occur even at larger boxes sizes, and it is a general phenomena of confined relativistic particles. And recent experiments on Graphene have reopened the debate [#klein3##1###]. Still, we will assume the traditional interpretation and that we can ignore the Klein paradox.
