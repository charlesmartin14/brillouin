Neutron Decay

By detailed balance, reverse electron capture has the same rate as orbital electron capture--but is more favorable energetically. Indeed, inside the nucleus, the neutron is relatively stable. Free neutron decay has mean lifetime of #math50##tex2html_wrap_inline969#, or about 15 minutes.

In contrast, orbital electron capture by a free proton is unspoken-of outside of a stellar environments. Even if the bare reaction could proceed, the reverse reaction would still dominate un less it is suppressed or is kinetically unfavorable.
